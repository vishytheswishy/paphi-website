Philanthropy

PI ALPHA PHI is deeply rooted in five pillars. With one of our pillars being philanthropy, we make it a mission to have our actions reflect positively towards the community. Sparking civic engagement, public opportunities, and empowering individuals are all actions that we aim to pursue. We hope that through our actions and words, PI ALPHA PHI will be a driving force to better our communiities.

Our Contributions to our community.

Vultus est index animi.

The new member class from the Fall of 2019 of Pi Alpha Phi has donated $367.50 towards the JED Foundation. This non-profit organization advocates for mental health and aims to further decrease the suicide rate amongst youths and young adults. In addition, the JED Foundation assists against drug abuse and offers services tailored towards suicide prevention, while also simultaneously coordinating with both high school and university demographics.
