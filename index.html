About Us

About Us

Pi Alpha Phi Fraternity, Inc. (ΠΑΦ, also Pi Alpha Phi or PAPhi) is an American university-level fraternity. It was founded in 1926 at the University of California, Berkeley, and is the oldest active Asian-American interest fraternity in the nation.

The Eta Chapter was officially established on September 16th, 1998 @ The University of California, Irvine.

Built on the principles formed and dictated in 1926, The Eta Chapter is here to serve our local community and provide leadership experience, brotherhood, and a platform for academic excellence. In addition, we stand against all forms of racial injustice and strive to achieve asian awareness in our community and beyond.
